Internship Day 5 Documentation

Day 5 Overview

On Day 5 of the internship, I explored the fundamentals of 3D printer characterization, 3D modelling workflows, and 3D scanning technologies. I learned how additive manufacturing systems are calibrated, tested, and optimized for accurate printing results.

3D Printer Characterization

  • Learned the basics of 3D printer calibration
  • Understood dimensional accuracy and print testing
  • Explored layer height, infill, and nozzle settings
  • Studied print quality optimization techniques

3D Printer Exploration

Explored the workflow and characterization process of 3D printers used in rapid prototyping and manufacturing.

3D Modelling & Scanning

  • Explored 3D modelling workflows and applications
  • Learned the basics of 3D scanning technology
  • Understood mesh generation and digital reconstruction
  • Studied practical applications of scanning in engineering

3D Scanning & Modelling Work

Learned how 3D scanning systems capture object geometry and convert it into digital 3D models for engineering, visualization, and manufacturing workflows.

Learning Outcome

Day 5 improved my understanding of additive manufacturing, digital fabrication, and 3D scanning workflows. I learned how modern prototyping technologies are used in engineering design, product development, and manufacturing.
